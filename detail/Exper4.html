Margroup

Margroup is a student-run marketing research group based at the University of Economics Ho Chi Minh City (UEH). Founded in 1994, Margroup is one of the largest, strongest, and longest-running student clubs/groups in the school. Our group's mission is to develop the skills of marketing students, create opportunities for students to intern and experience a professional work environment.

THINGS I DO

Contribution

I am a member of the Margroup Communications team. I am responsible for creating and managing the group's social media accounts, as well as writing and editing content for the group's website and newsletter. I also help to plan and execute the group's marketing campaigns. I am passionate about marketing and communication, and I am excited to be a part of a team that is dedicated to helping students develop their skills in these areas. I am also grateful for the opportunity to learn from experienced professionals and to collaborate with talented and motivated students.

Achievement

I have participated in "Explore Dynamic 2023: Take a Step" project as a member of communication team. The "Explore Dynamic 2023: Take a Step" event was a successful event that reached 10,000 audiences. The event organizers created engaging content that captured the attention of their target audience. This content included videos, infographics, and social media posts. Furthermore, the event organizers also used a variety of marketing channels to reach their target audience, including social media, offline marketing, activation day and talkshow.

What I learned from this job position

As a member of the Communications team, I have learned a lot about social media marketing, content writing, and event planning. I have also had the opportunity to meet and network with many people in the field of marketing industry. next challenge.
